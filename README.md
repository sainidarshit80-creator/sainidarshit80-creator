## About

I'm a 2nd-year Computer Science student at IIIT Vadodara, working at the intersection of **data, design, and systems**. My interests span machine learning, geospatial data, UI/UX, and quantitative finance, not because I haven't chosen yet, but because the most interesting problems tend to sit exactly where these fields collide.

I operate across disciplines that rarely talk to each other: I've processed satellite imagery for government infrastructure, written a research paper on how regional dialects shape generational identity, built commercial 3D designs, and spent quiet evenings thinking about what the night sky can tell us if treated as a dataset.

What drives me is building things that are both technically sound and genuinely useful to people.
